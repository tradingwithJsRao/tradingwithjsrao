Group Membership Enrollment and Psychometric Test Guidelines

Thank you for your interest in joining our group. To ensure a seamless enrollment process, please follow the steps outlined below:

Step 1: Enrollment and Payment

  1. Choose a group plan that suits your needs and proceed with the payment.
  2. Once payment is made, please send a screenshot of the payment confirmation, along with your name and email address, to our designated contact.

Step 2: Psychometric Test Access

Upon receiving your payment confirmation and required information, we will provide access to our psychometric test.

Step 3: Test Attempt and Evaluation

  1. Attempt the psychometric test within the allotted timeframe.
  2. Our evaluation team will review your test results.
  3. If you pass the test, we will grant you access to our group.

Appeal Process and Refund Policy

  1. If you fail the test, you will be given the opportunity to appeal the decision.
  2. If you fail in the appeal, then we will refund your fee.
  3. Important Note: If you pay the enrollment fee but do not appear in the test due to any reason, the fee will be non-refundable.

By following these guidelines, you can ensure a smooth enrollment process and successful integration into our group. If you have any questions or concerns, please do not hesitate to contact us.
